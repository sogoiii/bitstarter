A website for college counselors to track their students progress applying for colleges. The counselor adds information on the application process for a school. Or if a school has created an application, then that can be given/applied to a student. Then the student can go in and have the counselor review the progress for the student. Provide updates and incouragment for them to complete it. This can allow colleges to streamline their application process by having access to students directly. This makes the school become more invovled with their students secondary school education. Additionally, it makes the student aware of what must be done by proving a todo for them.

Create application process and TODOs for a college. Provide centralized location for counselors to advise and review student progress with their applications. For colleges, we provide an anonomozed rating to these completed/incompleted applications. Want to see a student has held a job? We scour all results and allow you to mark students as interesting. You could provide incouragment and extra advice. Maybe invite them to a campus tour and facilitate a relationship to attact students.

FAQ

Schools already have counselors who mange students progress. Why use this application?
True, but they only know as much as the student is willing to provide. This means if a student has lied about what they have done, only they will know.
So they know how much of an application a student has filled, so what?
A counselor now has actionable information. If a student is laking in recommendations, he/she can provide suggestions. If a student has missing sections, they can help complete them. Knowledge is power
College applications are done online now, how does this work?
Current versions only allow for the creation of the application, actually going to their college of choice and submitting information is done by them. In the future we want to have direct communication with all colleges so as to allow students to simply press a button and be done with it.
Why would a college want this at all?
More students applying allows them to be more selective.
What other benefits to colleges get?
Anonomized information on students interest in their college. Colleges can create trigger words/criteria that will analyize student applications. We provide a grading on how well a student matches the desires for the college.
